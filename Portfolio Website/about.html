About

Hi! I'm Reverie. I am an Art and Technology major at The Ohio State University. I love to work with all mediums, but due to my inspiration from cinema and animation, I plan to move forward with my experimentation in 2D and 3D animation.
My work is varied in every way, from theme, to material, to quality, to time spent, but overall, I want my work to tell the story of me: things I like, memories I cherish, my growth as an artist and a person, and my hopes and dreams for the future. I embrace change, allowing my art to reflect how I change along with the world around me.

Reverie Nedde is based in Boston, Massachusetts, and Columbus, Ohio. She is a recipient of the Fergus Portfolio Review Merit-based Scholarship, has been a member of the Dean's List for all semesters, is a three-time Academic All-American, and is an OSU Scholar Athlete.
