Self Care

2022

Artist Statement

Self Care is an animation that visualizes my own experiences with mental health, anxiety, and intrusive thoughts. In this work I use a technique called rotoscoping, where I took videos of myself doing certain actions, and traced over them frame by frame to create a moving image. I then took those clips further by adding more imagery on top, to give it a different meaning. “L'appel du vide”, or “call of the void” is a very frequent experience for me, where I’ll have vivid visualizations of things happening in my everyday life going terribly wrong, usually ending in self mutilation. I decided to use rotoscoping to depict these actions because though it’s disconnected from reality, an uncanny realistic feel still remains, reflecting the thoughts in my head.
